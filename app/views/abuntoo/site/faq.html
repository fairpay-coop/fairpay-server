Frequently Asked Questions

What is Abuntoo?

Abuntoo is a philanthropy platform that embodies the Gift Economy in action. When we share our abundance with others, we are expressing our humanity - the spirit of “ubuntu” - and the virtue of kindness. On Abuntoo , you can donate to any non-profit and enjoy gifts from merchants eager to share their offerings with you. We bring together the business and non-profit sectors in a meaningful way that is delightful to the conscientious donor / consumer - you!

What are Abuntoo Rewards?

While Abuntoo merchants all have their favorite causes to support, most of them have also agreed to provide thank-you gifts for donations to other charities. Therefore you can enjoy Abuntoo Rewards on this Abuntoo-powered campaign. We hope to help you discover new products and services offered by our merchant partners. Please be generous with your feedback and public reviews!

Is my transaction secure?

Yes, your transaction is secure! Abuntoo uses industry standard bank-level encryption between our servers and our payment processors. If you choose to save your payment information, we store a special authorization token we receive from our processor that can only be used by us. We do not store your credit card number or security code, but we do keep the last 4 digits and expiration date to make it easier for you to identify what card you used.
